Odin's Fried Rice Soup Recipe

Description

Another favourite and classic tasty meal. Perfect for using up that day old rice and any other left over vegatables you have left!

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp vegatable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 15g butter
  • 2 Tbsp soy suace

Steps

  1. Bring a wok or large pan to a high heat with the oil. Once heated, add the rice and garlic, then stir fry for 4-5 minutes.
  2. Push the rice to the side of the pan, and pour the beaten egg into the empty side. Stir to scramble, then stir through the rice.
  3. Pour the soy sauce, oyster sauce and sesame oil into the rice. Stir to combine, then fold through the frozen vegetables and cook for 3-4 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Add the butter and stir until melted, then serve immediately.
